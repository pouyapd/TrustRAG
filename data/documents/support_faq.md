# Acme Cloud — Support and Security FAQ

## Account Recovery

If you forget your password, click "Forgot password" on the login screen. A reset link will be sent to your registered email and is valid for 30 minutes.

If you lose access to your email, contact support@acmecloud.example with proof of identity. Account recovery without email access takes 3–5 business days.

## Data Backup

Pro and Enterprise plans include automatic daily backups retained for 30 days. Starter plan accounts can manually export their data at any time but do not receive automatic backups.

## Two-Factor Authentication

Two-factor authentication (2FA) is available on all plans and is mandatory for Enterprise accounts. We support TOTP authenticator apps and hardware keys (FIDO2).

## Outage Notifications

Service status is published in real time at status.acmecloud.example. Enterprise customers receive direct email notifications during incidents that affect their region.
