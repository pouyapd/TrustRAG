# Acme Cloud — Refund and Cancellation Policy

## Refund Eligibility

Customers on monthly plans are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of their initial purchase. Annual subscribers have a 30-day refund window from the date of purchase.

## How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email billing@acmecloud.example with your order ID. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days and returned to the original payment method.

## Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from the Billing tab in your account settings. Cancellation stops future charges but does not retroactively refund the current billing period unless the request falls within the eligibility window above.

## Exceptions

Refunds do not apply to add-on services (such as priority support packs or one-time storage upgrades) once they have been activated. Refunds are also not available for accounts that have violated the Acceptable Use Policy.
