# Acme Cloud — Product Specifications

## Plan Tiers

Acme Cloud offers three plan tiers: Starter, Pro, and Enterprise.

The Starter plan includes 10 GB of storage, 1 user seat, and email support. It costs 9 EUR per month or 90 EUR per year.

The Pro plan includes 100 GB of storage, 5 user seats, and priority email support with response within 24 hours. It costs 29 EUR per month or 290 EUR per year.

The Enterprise plan includes 1 TB of storage, unlimited user seats, dedicated account manager, and 99.9% uptime SLA. Enterprise pricing is custom and requires contacting sales.

## Regions

Acme Cloud data centers are located in Frankfurt, Dublin, and Milan within the EU. All data is stored within the EU and never leaves the region selected at signup.

## Compliance

Acme Cloud is GDPR-compliant and ISO 27001 certified. The platform supports SSO via SAML 2.0 on Pro and Enterprise plans only.

## API Limits

The Pro plan allows 1000 API requests per minute. The Enterprise plan allows 10000 requests per minute. Starter plan accounts cannot access the API.
